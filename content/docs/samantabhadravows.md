**Avatamsaka Sutra 36, Taishō 279**

#### 所有十方世界中  
#### 三世一切人師子  
#### 我以清凈身語意  
#### 一切遍禮盡無余

Suǒyǒu shí fāng shìjiè zhōng sānshì yīqiè rén shī zi 

wǒ yǐ qīngjìng shēn yǔyì yīqiè biàn lǐ jìn wú yú

Body, speech and mind, purified, in oneness,

I bow deeply to touch limitless Buddhas

of the past, present, and future

throughout all worlds in the Ten Directions.

#### 普賢行願威神力  
#### 普現一切如來前  
#### 一身覆現剎塵身  
#### 一一遍禮剎塵佛

Pǔ xián xíng yuàn wēi shénlì pǔ xiàn yīqiè rúlái qián yīshēn fù xiàn shā chén shēn yīyī biàn lǐ shā chén fú

The power of Samantabhadra’s vow

enables me to be present everywhere.

Where there is a Buddha, I am there.

As Buddhas are countless, so too am I.

#### 於一塵中塵數佛  
#### 各處菩薩眾會中  
#### 無盡法界塵亦然  
#### 深信諸佛皆充滿

Yú yī chén zhōng chén shù fú gè chù púsà zhòng huì zhōng wújìn fǎjiè chén yì rán shēnxìn zhū fú jiē chōngmǎn

In a particle of dust are countless Buddhas,

all of them present with their own assembly.

The strength of my faith penetrates deeply

into every atom of all Dharma realms.


#### 各以一切音聲海  
#### 普出無盡妙言辭  
#### 盡於未來一切劫  
#### 讚佛甚深功德海

Gè yǐ yīqiè yīn shēng hǎi pǔ chū wújìn miào yáncí jǐn yú wèilái yīqiè jié zàn fú shèn shēn gōngdé hǎi

I aspire to use the Great Ocean of Sound,

giving rise to words of wonderful effect

that praise the Buddha’s oceans of virtues,

in the past, present, and future.

#### 以諸最勝妙華鬘  
#### 伎樂塗香及傘蓋  
#### 如是最勝莊嚴具  
#### 我以供養諸如來

Yǐ zhū zuì shèng miào huámán jì lè tú xiāng jí sǎn gài rúshì zuì shèng zhuāngyán jù wǒ yǐ gòngyǎng zhūrú lái

I bring these offerings:

garlands of the most beautiful flowers,

incense, music, perfumes, and parasols,

all to adorn the Tathagatas and their lands.

#### 最勝衣服最勝香  
#### 末香燒香與燈燭  
#### 一一皆如妙高聚  
#### 我悉供養諸如來

Zuì shèng yīfú zuì shèng xiāng mò xiāng shāoxiāng yǔ dēng zhú yīyī jiē rú miàogāo jù wǒ xī gòngyǎng zhūrú lái

Bringing food, robes, and fragrant flowers,

torches, sandalwood, sitting mats,

the finest adornments here in abundance –

an offering to the Tathagatas.

#### 我以廣大勝解心  
#### 深信一切三世佛  
#### 悉以普賢行願力  
#### 普遍供養諸如來

Wǒ yǐ guǎngdà shèng jiě xīn shēnxìn yīqiè sānshì fú xī yǐ pǔ xián xíng yuàn lì pǔbiàn gòngyǎng zhūrú lái

Inspired by Samantabhadra’s vow,

I bring my heart, wide with deep understanding,

with loving faith in the Buddhas of the Three Times,

as an offering to the Tathagatas everywhere.

#### 我昔所造諸惡業  
#### 皆由無始貪嗔癡  
#### 從身語意之所生  
#### 一切我今皆懺悔

Wǒ xī suǒ zào zhū è yè jiē yóu wú shǐ tān chēn chī cóng shēn yǔyì zhī suǒ shēng yīqiè wǒ jīn jiē chànhuǐ

From beginningless time I have acted unskillfully

with craving, hatred, and ignorance

in actions of body, speech, and mind.

Determined now to begin anew, I repent.


#### 十方一切諸眾生  
#### 二乘有學及無學  
#### 一切如來與菩薩  
#### 所有功德皆隨喜

Shí fāng yīqiè zhū zhòngshēng èr chéng yǒu xué jí wú xué yīqiè rúlái yǔ púsà suǒyǒu gōngdé jiē suíxǐ

All sentient beings in the ten directions have learning and non-learning of the two vehicles, all Tathagatas and Bodhisattvas, all merits and virtues rejoice

#### 十方所有世間燈  
#### 最初成就菩提者  
#### 我今一切皆勸請  
#### 轉於無上妙法輪

Shí fāng suǒyǒu shìjiān dēng zuìchū chéngjiù pútí zhě wǒ jīn yīqiè jiē quàn qǐng zhuǎn yú wú shàng miào fǎlún

I rejoice in every virtuous action

by anyone, in any direction,

by learners and by those who need learn no more,

of Buddhas and Bodhisattvas.


#### 諸佛若欲示涅槃  
#### 我悉至誠而勸請  
#### 唯願久住剎塵劫  
#### 利樂一切諸眾生

Zhū fú ruò yù shì nièpán wǒ xī zhìchéng ér quàn qǐng wéi yuàn jiǔzhù shā chén jié lì lè yīqiè zhū zhòngshēng

If all the buddhas wish to show Nirvana, I sincerely advise you, I only wish to abide for a long time for the benefit of all sentient beings.

#### 所有禮讚供養福  
#### 請佛住世轉法輪  
#### 隨喜懺悔諸善根  
#### 回向眾生及佛道

Suǒyǒu lǐzàn gòngyǎng fú qǐng fú zhùshì zhuǎn fǎlún suíxǐ chànhuǐ zhū shàngēn huí xiàng zhòngshēng jí fú dào

All praises, offerings, blessings, ask the Buddha to live in the world and turn the wheel of Dharma, repent with joy and repent of all good roots, and dedicate to all beings and the Buddha’s way

#### 我隨一切如來學  
#### 修習普賢圓滿行  
#### 供養過去諸如來  
#### 及與現在十方佛

Wǒ suí yīqiè rúlái xué xiūxí pǔ xián yuánmǎn xíng gòngyǎng guòqù zhūrú lái jí yǔ xiànzài shí fāng fú

I follow all the Tathagatas to study, to practice Samantabhadra's perfect behavior, to make offerings to the past Tathagatas and to the present Buddhas of the ten directions

#### 未來一切天人師  
#### 一切意樂皆圓滿  
#### 我願普隨三世學  
#### 速得成就大菩提

Wèilái yīqiè tiān rén shī yīqiè yì lè jiē yuánmǎn wǒ yuàn pǔ suí sānshì xué sù dé chéngjiù dà pútí

In the future, all heavenly and human teachers, all desires and pleasures will be fulfilled, I wish to follow the three worlds of study, and quickly achieve great Bodhi

#### 所有十方一切剎  
#### 廣大清凈妙莊嚴  
#### 眾會圍繞諸如來  
#### 悉在菩提樹王下

Suǒyǒu shí fāng yīqiè shā guǎngdà qīngjìng miào zhuāngyán zhòng huì wéirào zhūrú lái xī zài pútíshù wáng xià

All the temples in the ten directions are vast, pure and wonderfully adorned, and the assembly will surround the Tathagata, all under the king of the Bodhi tree

#### 十方所有諸眾生  
#### 願離憂患常安樂  
#### 獲得甚深正法利  
#### 滅除煩惱盡無余

Shí fāng suǒyǒu zhū zhòngshēng yuàn lí yōuhuàn cháng ānlè huòdé shèn shēn zhèngfǎ lì miè chú fánnǎo jìn wú yú

All sentient beings in the ten directions may be free from sorrows and afflictions and always be happy, obtain profound and righteous Dharma benefits, and eradicate all afflictions.

#### 我為菩提修行時  
#### 一切趣中成宿命  
#### 常得出家修凈戒  
#### 無垢無破無穿漏

Wǒ wèi pútí xiūxíng shí yīqiè qù zhōng chéng xiǔ mìng cháng dé chūjiā xiū jìng jiè wúgòu wú pò wú chuān lòu

When I practise for Bodhi, I will achieve my destiny in all realms, I will often go home and cultivate the pure precepts, without any blemish, without breaking, and without penetrating leakage.

#### 天龍夜叉鳩槃荼  
#### 乃至人與非人等  
#### 所有一切眾生語  
#### 悉以諸音而說法

Tiānlóng yèchā jiū pán tú nǎizhì rén yǔ fēi rén děng suǒyǒu yīqiè zhòngshēng yǔ xī yǐ zhū yīn ér shuōfǎ

Heavenly dragon, Yaksha, Dovepanta, even human beings and non-human beings, all the speeches of all beings are spoken in various sounds.

#### 勤修清凈波羅密  
#### 恒不忘失菩提心  
#### 滅除障垢無有余  
#### 一切妙行皆成就

Qín xiū qīngjìng bō luó mì héng bù wàngshī pútíxīn miè chú zhàng gòu wú yǒuyú yīqiè miào xíng jiē chéngjiù

Diligently practise the pure Paramita, never forget the bodhicitta, eliminate obstacles and stains, there is nothing left, all wonderful deeds are accomplished

#### 於諸惑業及魔境  
#### 世間道中得解脫  
#### 猶如蓮華不著水  
#### 亦如日月不住空

Yú zhū huò yè jí mójìng shì jiàn dàozhōng dé jiětuō yóurú liánhuá bùzháo shuǐ yì rú rì yuè bù zhù kōng

To be liberated from the world of delusions and demons, like a lotus flower without water, like the sun and the moon without space

#### 悉除一切惡道苦  
#### 等與一切群生樂  
#### 如是經於剎塵劫  
#### 十方利益恒無盡

Xī chú yīqiè è dào kǔ děng yǔ yīqiè qúnshēng lè rúshì jīng yú shā chén jié shí fāng lìyì héng wújìn

All the sufferings of the evil realms are eliminated, and all living beings are happy. Thus, the benefits of the ten directions are endless.

#### 我常隨順諸眾生  
#### 盡於未來一切劫  
#### 恒修普賢廣大行  
#### 圓滿無上大菩提

Wǒ cháng suíshùn zhū zhòngshēng jǐn yú wèilái yīqiè jié héng xiū pǔ xián guǎngdà xíng yuánmǎn wú shàng dà pútí

I will always follow all sentient beings for all the future kalpas, constantly cultivate the vast deeds of Samantabhadra, and consummate the supreme bodhi


#### 所有與我同行者  
#### 於一切處同集會  
#### 身口意業皆同等  
#### 一切行願同修學

Suǒyǒu yǔ wǒ tóngxíng zhě yú yīqiè chù tóng jíhuì shēnkǒuyì yè jiē tóngděng yīqiè xíng yuàn tóng xiū xué

All those who walk with me assemble together everywhere with the same body, speech, mind, and deeds, all deeds and aspirations to learn from fellow practitioners


#### 所有益我善知識  
#### 為我顯示普賢行  
#### 常願與我同集會  
#### 於我常生歡喜心

Suǒyǒu yì wǒ shàn zhīshì wèi wǒ xiǎnshì pǔ xián xíng cháng yuàn yǔ wǒ tóng jíhuì yú wǒ chángshēng huānxǐ xīn

All those who benefit me, show me the virtues of Samantabhadra, always wish to gather with me, and always have a happy heart in me


#### 願常面見諸如來  
#### 及諸佛子眾圍繞  
#### 於彼皆興廣大供  
#### 盡未來劫無疲厭

Yuàn cháng miàn jiàn zhū rúlái jí zhū fú zi zhòng wéirào yú bǐ jiē xìng guǎngdà gōng jǐn wèilái jié wú pí yàn

May I often see the Tathagata and all the disciples of the Buddha around, and make great offerings to them, and never get tired of the future kalpas


#### 願持諸佛微妙法  
#### 光顯一切菩提行  
#### 究竟清凈普賢道  
#### 盡未來劫常修習

Yuàn chí zhū fú wéimiào fǎ guāng xiǎn yīqiè pútí háng jiùjìng qīngjìng pǔ xián dào jǐn wèilái jié cháng xiūxí

I wish to uphold the subtle Dharma of all Buddhas to reveal all Bodhisattva practices, the ultimate purity of the Samantabhadra Path, and to practise it throughout the future kalpas

#### 我於一切諸有中  
#### 所修福智恒無盡  
#### 定慧方便及解脫  
#### 獲諸無盡功德藏

Wǒ yú yīqiè zhū yǒu zhòng suǒ xiū fú zhì héng wújìn dìng huì fāngbiàn jí jiětuō huò zhū wújìn gōngdé cáng

In all beings, the blessings and wisdom I have cultivated are inexhaustible, steadfast, wisdom, convenience, and liberation, and I have obtained inexhaustible treasures of merit.

#### 一塵中有塵數剎  
#### 一一剎有難思佛  
#### 一一佛處眾會中  
#### 我見恒演菩提行

Yī chén zhōng yǒu chén shù shā yīyī chà yǒunán sī fú yīyī fú chù zhòng huì zhōng wǒ jiàn héng yǎn pútí háng

There are as many motes of dust in a single dust, and in a single moment, it is difficult to think about the Buddha. The Buddha is in the assembly. I see the constant performance of Bodhi.

#### 普盡十方諸剎海  
#### 一一毛端三世海  
#### 佛海及與國土海  
#### 我遍修行經劫海

Pǔ jǐn shí fāng zhū shā hǎi yīyī máo duān sānshì hǎi fú hǎi jí yǔ guótǔ hǎi wǒ biàn xiūxíng jīng jié hǎi

Exhausting all the seas of the ten directions, the sea of Maoduan III, the sea of Buddhas and the sea of land

#### 一切如來語清凈  
#### 一言具眾音聲海  
#### 隨諸眾生意樂音  
#### 一一流佛辯才海

Yīqiè rúlái yǔ qīngjìng yī yán jù zhòng yīn shēng hǎi suí zhū zhòngshēng yì yuèyīn yī yīliú fú biàncái hǎi

All the words of the Tathagata are pure, one word contains a sea of voices, a sea of sounds of business, a sea of first-class Buddha eloquence


#### 三世一切諸如來  
#### 於彼無盡語言海  
#### 恒轉理趣妙法輪  
#### 我深智力普能入

Sānshì yīqiè zhūrú lái yú bǐ wújìn yǔyán hǎi héng zhuǎn lǐ qù miào fǎlún wǒ shēn zhìlì pǔ néng rù

All the things of the three worlds come from the endless sea of language, the constant transference of the wonderful Falun, my deep intellect and universal access


#### 我能深入於未來  
#### 盡一切劫為一念  
#### 三世所有一切劫  
#### 為一念際我皆入

Wǒ néng shēnrù yú wèilái jǐn yīqiè jié wéi yīniàn sānshì suǒyǒu yīqiè jié wéi yīniàn jì wǒ jiē rù

I can go deep into the future and do all the kalpas as one thought

#### 我於一念見三世  
#### 所有一切人師子  
#### 亦常入佛境界中  
#### 如幻解脫及威力

Wǒ yú yīniàn jiàn sānshì suǒyǒu yīqiè rén shī zi yì cháng rù fó jìngjiè zhōng rú huàn jiětuō jí wēilì

In a single thought, I saw that all people, teachers, and students in the three worlds are always entering the realm of the Buddha, such as illusory liberation and power.

#### 於一毛端極微中  
#### 出現三世莊嚴剎  
#### 十方塵剎諸毛端  
#### 我皆深入而嚴凈

Yú yī máo duān jíwēi zhòng chūxiàn sānshì zhuāngyán shā shí fāng chén shā zhū máo duān wǒ jiē shēnrù ér yán jìng

In a very tiny hair tip, the three-generation majestic temples appear. All hair tips in the dust temples of the ten directions are deep and pure.

#### 所有未來照世燈  
#### 成道轉法悟群有  
#### 究竟佛事示涅槃  
#### 我皆往詣而親近

Suǒyǒu wèilái zhào shì dēng chéngdào zhuǎn fǎ wù qún yǒu jiùjìng fóshì shì nièpán wǒ jiē wǎng yì ér qīnjìn

All the lights in the future will illuminate the world, become enlightened, turn to the Dharma, and enlighten the group. After all, the Buddha's deeds will show Nirvana. I will all go there and get close.

#### 速疾周遍神通力  
#### 普門遍入大乘力  
#### 智行普修功德力  
#### 威神普覆大慈力

Sù jí zhōu biàn shéntōnglì pǔ mén biàn rù dàchéng lì zhìxíng pǔ xiū gōngdé lì wēi shén pǔ fù dàcí lì

The power of supernatural power, the power of universal access to the Mahayana, the power of wisdom and practice, the power of universal cultivation, the power of great compassion

#### 遍凈莊嚴勝福力  
#### 無著無依智慧力  
#### 定慧方便威神力  
#### 普能積集菩提力

Biàn jìng zhuāngyán shèng fú lì wúzhuó wú yī zhìhuì lì dìng huì fāngbiàn wēi shénlì pǔ néng jī jí pútí lì

The power of universal purity and adornment, the power of victory, the power of wisdom without attachment, the power of wisdom, the power of wisdom, the power of convenience, the power of universal energy, the power of accumulating bodhi

  
#### 清凈一切善業力  
#### 摧滅一切煩惱力  
#### 降服一切諸魔力  
#### 圓滿普賢諸行力

Qīngjìng yīqiè shàn yè lì cuī miè yīqiè fánnǎo lì xiángfú yīqiè zhū mólì yuánmǎn pǔ xián zhū xíng lì

Purify all good karma, destroy all afflictions, subdue all magical powers, and perfect all virtues

#### 普能嚴凈諸剎海  
#### 解脫一切眾生海  
#### 善能分別諸法海  
#### 能甚深入智慧海

Pǔ néng yán jìng zhū shā hǎi jiětuō yīqiè zhòngshēng hǎi shàn néng fēnbié zhū fǎ hǎi néng shèn shēnrù zhìhuì hǎi

Universally able to purify the seas of all realms, liberate all sentient beings from the sea, good at distinguishing the sea of dharmas, able to penetrate deeply into the sea of wisdom


#### 普能清凈諸行海  
#### 圓滿一切諸願海  
#### 親近供養諸佛海  
#### 修行無倦經劫海

Pǔ néng qīngjìng zhū xíng hǎi yuánmǎn yīqiè zhū yuàn hǎi qīnjìn gòngyǎng zhū fú hǎi xiūxíng wú juàn jīng jié hǎi

Universally able to purify the sea of actions, to complete all the sea of vows, to be close to the sea of offerings to the Buddhas, to practice the sea of untiring sutras


#### 三世一切諸如來  
#### 最勝菩提諸行願  
#### 我皆供養圓滿修  
#### 以普賢行悟菩提

Sānshì yīqiè zhūrú lái zuì shèng pútí zhū xíng yuàn wǒ jiē gòngyǎng yuánmǎn xiū yǐ pǔ xián xíng wù pútí

All the Tathagatas in the three worlds, the most excellent Bodhi, all the vows, I will make offerings to complete the cultivation, and realize Bodhi with Samantabhadra


#### 一切如來有長子  
#### 彼名號曰普賢尊  
#### 我今回向諸善根  
#### 願諸智行悉同彼

Yīqiè rúlái yǒu zhǎngzǐ bǐ mínghào yuē pǔ xián zūn wǒ jīnhuí xiàng zhū shàngēn yuàn zhū zhìxíng xī tóng bǐ

All Tathagatas have the eldest son. His name is Puxianzun. I am now devoting myself to the roots of goodness. May all wisdom and deeds be with him.

#### 願身口意恒清凈  
#### 諸行剎土亦覆然  
#### 如是智慧號普賢  
#### 願我與彼皆同等

Yuàn shēnkǒuyì héng qīngjìng zhū xíng shā tǔ yì fù rán rúshì zhìhuì hào pǔ xián yuàn wǒ yǔ bǐ jiē tóngděng

May the body, speech and mind be always pure, and the realms of all actions are also covered. Such wisdom is called Samantabhadra. May I be equal to them.

#### 我為遍凈普賢行  
#### 文殊師利諸大願  
#### 滿彼事業盡無余  
#### 未來際劫恒無倦

Wǒ wèi biàn jìng pǔ xián xíng wénshū shī lì zhū dàyuàn mǎn bǐ shìyè jìn wú yú wèilái jì jié héng wú juàn

I act for the universal and pure Samantabhadra, Manjushri benefit all the great wishes, fulfill his career and have no end, the future will be eternal and tireless

#### 我所修行無有量  
#### 獲得無量諸功德  
#### 安住無量諸行中  
#### 了達一切神通力

Wǒ suǒ xiūxíng wú yǒu liàng huòdé wúliàng zhū gōngdé ānzhù wúliàng zhū xíng zhōng le dá yīqiè shéntōnglì

I have practiced immeasurably, gained immeasurable merits, settled in immeasurable actions, and achieved all supernatural powers

#### 文殊師利勇猛智  
#### 普賢慧行亦覆然  
#### 我今回向諸善根  
#### 隨彼一切常修學

Wénshū shī lì yǒngměng zhì pǔ xiánhuì xíng yì fù rán wǒ jīnhuí xiàng zhū shàngēn suí bǐ yīqiè cháng xiū xué

Manjushri is brave and wise, and the wisdom of Puxian is also overturned. Now I return to the roots of goodness and follow him to learn all the time.

#### 三世諸佛所稱嘆  
#### 如是最勝諸大願  
#### 我今回向諸善根  
#### 為得普賢殊勝行

Sānshì zhū fú suǒ chēngtàn rúshì zuì shèng zhū dàyuàn wǒ jīnhuí xiàng zhū shàngēn wéi dé pǔ xián shūshèng xíng

The Buddhas of the three times have praised such a great vow. I am now devoting myself to the roots of goodness in order to obtain the supreme deed of the universal sage.


#### 願我離欲命終時  
#### 盡除一切諸障礙  
#### 面見彼佛阿彌陀  
#### 即得往生安樂剎

Yuàn wǒ lí yù mìng zhōng shí jǐn chú yīqiè zhū zhàng'ài miàn jiàn bǐ fú ēmítuó jí dé wǎngshēng ānlè shā

May I get rid of all obstacles at the end of my life, and meet the Buddha Amitabha, and I will be reborn in the realm of peace and happiness.

#### 我既往生彼國已  
#### 現前成就此大願  
#### 一切圓滿盡無余  
#### 利樂一切眾生界

Wǒ jìwǎng shēng bǐ guó yǐ xiàn qián chéngjiù cǐ dàyuàn yī qiē yuánmǎn jìn wú yú lì lè yīqiè zhòngshēng jiè

I have been born in that country, and now I have fulfilled this great wish, everything is perfect, and there is no remainder, benefiting all sentient beings

#### 彼佛眾會鹹清凈  
#### 我時於勝蓮華生  
#### 親睹如來無量光  
#### 現前授我菩提記

Bǐ fú zhòng huì xián qīngjìng wǒ shí yú shèng liánhuá shēng qīn dǔ rúlái wúliàng guāng xiàn qián shòu wǒ pútí jì

The assembly of the Buddha was pure and clean. When I was born in the lotus flower in victory, I saw the immeasurable light of the Tathagata and taught me the Bodhi Book.

#### 蒙彼如來授記已  
#### 化身無數百俱胝  
#### 智力廣大遍十方  
#### 普利一切眾生界

Méng bǐ rúlái shòujì yǐ huàshēn wú shù bǎi jù zhī zhìlì guǎngdà biàn shí fāng pǔ lì yīqiè zhòngshēng jiè

After being prophesied by the Tathagata, he has incarnated without hundreds of corpses, his intellect is vast and spread throughout the ten directions, and all sentient beings are in the world.

#### 乃至虛空世界盡  
#### 眾生及業煩惱盡  
#### 如是一切無盡時  
#### 我願究竟恒無盡

Nǎizhì xūkōng shìjiè jǐn zhòngshēng jí yè fánnǎo jǐn rúshì yīqiè wújìn shí wǒ yuàn jiùjìng héng wújìn

Even when the empty world is exhausted, sentient beings and karmic afflictions are exhausted, and when everything is endless, I wish to be eternal.

#### 十方所有無邊剎  
#### 莊嚴眾寶供如來  
#### 最勝安樂施天人  
#### 經一切剎微塵劫

Shí fāng suǒyǒu wúbiān shā zhuāngyán zhòng bǎo gōng rúlái zuì shèng ānlè shī tiān rén jīng yīqiè shā wéichén jié

All the boundless temples in the ten directions Adorn the many treasures for the Tathagata, the most prosperous and peaceful, the gods and people of Oxygen, who have gone through all the motes of dust and kalpas

#### 若人於此勝願王  
#### 一經於耳能生信  
#### 求勝菩提心渴仰  
#### 獲勝功德過於彼

Ruò rén yú cǐ shèng yuàn wáng yījīng yú ěr néngshēng xìn qiú shèng pútíxīn kě yǎng huòshèng gōngdé guòyú bǐ

If a person here, the King of Victory Vow, can generate faith in his ears, and the Bodhicitta desire for victory, the merit of victory is greater than that of him.


#### 即常遠離惡知識  
#### 永離一切諸惡道  
#### 速見如來無量光  
#### 具此普賢最勝願

Jí cháng yuǎnlí è zhīshì yǒng lí yīqiè zhū è dào sùjiàn rúlái wúliàng guāng jù cǐ pǔ xián zuì shèng yuàn

That is, always stay away from evil knowledge, stay away from all evil paths forever, and see the immeasurable light of the Tathagata quickly.

#### 此人善得勝壽命  
#### 此人善來人中生  
#### 此人不久當成就  
#### 如彼普賢菩薩行

Cǐ rén shàn déshèng shòumìng cǐ rén shàn lái rén zhōng shēng cǐ rén bùjiǔ dàng chéngjiù rú bǐ pǔ xián púsà xíng

This person is good at conquering lifespan. This person is good at rebirth among other people. This person will soon become successful like Samantabhadra Bodhisattva.

#### 往昔由無智慧力  
#### 所造極惡五無間  
#### 誦此普賢大願王  
#### 一念速疾皆消滅

Wǎngxī yóu wú zhìhuì lì suǒ zào jí è wǔ wújiàn sòng cǐ pǔ xián dàyuàn wáng yīniàn sù jí jiē xiāomiè

In the past, the worst evils were created by the power of lack of wisdom, and the five endless recitations of this Samantabhadra Great Vow King will be wiped out with a single thought.

#### 族姓種類及容色  
#### 相好智慧鹹圓滿  
#### 諸魔外道不能摧  
#### 堪為三界所應供

Zú xìng zhǒnglèi jí róng sè xiāng hǎo zhìhuì xián yuánmǎn zhū mó wàidào bùnéng cuī kān wèi sānjiè suǒ yīng gōng

Kind of family name and appearance, good looks, wisdom and perfection, all demons and outsiders cannot be destroyed, they can be supplied by the three realms

#### 速詣菩提大樹王  
#### 坐已降服諸魔眾  
#### 成等正覺轉法輪  
#### 普利一切諸含識

Sù yì pútí dàshù wáng zuò yǐ xiángfú zhū mó zhòng chéng děng zhèng jué zhuǎn fǎlún pǔ lì yīqiè zhū hán shí

Speed up to the King of the Bodhi tree, I have already surrendered to all the demons, I have attained perfect enlightenment, I turn the wheel of Dharma, and I have all the comprehensions.

#### 若人於此普賢願  
#### 讀誦受持及演說  
#### 果報唯佛能證知  
#### 決定獲勝菩提道

Ruò rén yú cǐ pǔ xián yuàn dú sòng shòu chí jí yǎnshuō guǒbào wéi fú néng zhèng zhī juédìng huòshèng pútí dào

If one wishes to read, accept, uphold and speak here, only the Buddha can realize the retribution and decide to win the Bodhi path.


#### 若人誦此普賢願  
#### 我說少分之善根  
#### 一念一切悉皆圓  
#### 成就眾生清凈願

Ruò rén sòng cǐ pǔ xián yuàn wǒ shuō shǎo fēn zhī shàngēn yīniàn yīqiè xījiē yuán chéngjiù zhòngshēng qīngjìng yuàn

If anyone recites this Samantabhadra Vow, I say that a small fraction of the good roots will be fulfilled, and all beings will be fulfilled.

#### 我此普賢殊勝行  
#### 無邊勝福皆回向  
#### 普願沈溺諸眾生  
#### 速往無量光佛剎

Wǒ cǐ pǔ xián shūshèng xíng wúbiān shèng fú jiē huí xiàng pǔyuàn shěnnì zhū zhòngshēng sù wǎng wúliàng guāng fú shā

I, this Samantabhadra’s extraordinary deeds, the boundless blessings are all dedicated to the universal wish to indulge all sentient beings, and quickly go to the Buddha-field of immeasurable light

#### 殊勝之最發願王  
#### 利益無邊諸眾生  
#### 圓滿普賢行願力  
#### 三惡道眾盡解脫

Shūshèng zhī zuì fāyuàn wáng lìyì wúbiān zhū zhòngshēng yuánmǎn pǔ xián xíng yuàn lì sān è dào zhòng jǐn jiětuō

The supreme and most aspirational king Benefit boundless sentient beings Complete the power of universal sages and vows to liberate all beings from the three evil paths

**The Ten Great Aspirations of Samantabhadra Bodhisattva**

Avatamsaka Sutra 36, Taishō 279

Body, speech and mind, purified, in oneness,

I bow deeply to touch limitless Buddhas

of the past, present, and future

throughout all worlds in the Ten Directions.

---

The power of Samantabhadra’s vow

enables me to be present everywhere.

Where there is a Buddha, I am there.

As Buddhas are countless, so too am I.

---

In a particle of dust are countless Buddhas,

all of them present with their own assembly.

The strength of my faith penetrates deeply

into every atom of all Dharma realms.

---

I aspire to use the Great Ocean of Sound,

giving rise to words of wonderful effect

that praise the Buddha’s oceans of virtues,

in the past, present, and future.

---

I bring these offerings:

garlands of the most beautiful flowers,

incense, music, perfumes, and parasols,

all to adorn the Tathagatas and their lands.

---

Bringing food, robes, and fragrant flowers,

torches, sandalwood, sitting mats,

the finest adornments here in abundance –

an offering to the Tathagatas.

---

Inspired by Samantabhadra’s vow,

I bring my heart, wide with deep understanding,

with loving faith in the Buddhas of the Three Times,

as an offering to the Tathagatas everywhere.

---

From beginningless time I have acted unskillfully

with craving, hatred, and ignorance

in actions of body, speech, and mind.

Determined now to begin anew, I repent.

---

I rejoice in every virtuous action

by anyone, in any direction,

by learners and by those who need learn no more,

of Buddhas and Bodhisattvas.

---

All beings who are Lamps for the world

and those who have just attained enlightenment,

I beg that you will think lovingly of us,

turning the Wheel of the Dharma to help the world.

---

With sincerity, I make a humble request

of the Buddhas and those who are about to enter nirvana:

remain forever in the world,

for the benefit and the welfare of all.

---

I humbly make offerings inviting all Buddhas

to stay with us and guide all beings to the other shore.

All the merit of joyous praise and repenting

I offer to the Path of Awakening.

---

This merit is transferred to the Three Jewels,

to their nature and form in the Dharma realms.

The Two Truths are perfectly woven together

into the Samadhi Seal.

---

The ocean of merit is measureless.

I vow to transfer it and not hold anything for myself.

If any human, out of discrimination and prejudice,

tries to do harm to the path of liberation

may their obstacles be fully removed.

---

In each moment, wisdom envelops the Dharma realms,

welcoming all to the place of non-regression.

Space and living beings are without limit,

the same with afflictions and results of past actions.

These four are fully and truly immeasurable.

So, too, is my offering of merit.

---

Translated by Thich Nhat Hanh from Avatamsaka Sutra 36, Taishō Revised Tripiṭaka 279, Flower Garland Discourse.
